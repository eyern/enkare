Our Services

Hydro Geological Survey

Enkare Drilling Ltd perfoms geological assessments that focuses on groundwater resources, including their location, depth, quality, and potential for sustainable use.

Drilling & Casing Installation.

Installing drilling casing as the hole is drilled helps to stabilize loose, or unpredictable formations, and shore up the hole.

Flushing

Cleaning a borehole by removing debris, cuttings, and drilling fluids to improve water flow and prevent contamination.

Test Pumping

Enkare Drilling Ltd a crucial assesses the yield and sustainability of a new or existing borehole, helping to determine the optimal pumping rate and assess the impact on surrounding groundwater resources.

Pump Installation

It includes assessing the borehole, selecting the appropriate pump, and ensuring proper installation and maintenance.

Borehole Equipping

Installing necessary equipments, like pumps and accessories, within a drilled borehole to create a functional water supply system.
